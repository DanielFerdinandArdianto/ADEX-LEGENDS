ADEX LEGENDS

ADEX LEGENDS is a battle royal shooter game that released in 2020. It available in PC, PS4, and Mobile. You can choose a hero that you want, every hero has a different skill and ability.


Arena

Arena is a team play that have a smaller places so you just need 15 minutes to finish this game. To win this game , you team have to kill all enemies and also it has like a playzone that become smaller and smaller.


Ranked

Ranked is similar to Arena, it is an original game mode but in this mode it takes a little bit longer times to play about 30 minutes. you also have to kill all enemies to win this game.


Overview

Adex Legends is an online games that give you a best experience of playing a multiplayer battle royale game.
